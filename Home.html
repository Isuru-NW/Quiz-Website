Ah, Disney World! So many colourful things to do and places to see here. Not enough time to describe. Where do I start?

This is well known as the Happiest place on the world and the Most magical place in the world..Here you can meet your favourite cartoon characters like most famous Mickey Mouse,Whinne the Poo,Cinderalla and many more..
